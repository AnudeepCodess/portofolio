Clinical Medical Imaging – Cardiac Segmentation

Developed deep learning left ventricular segmentation models for pediatric echocardiogram DICOM images using EchoNet Dynamic, supporting clinically relevant cardiac measurements.

Technology Stack

  • ML & DL: PyTorch, Computer Vision
  • Cloud & MLOps: Vertex AI, Kubeflow, Docker, Cloud Build, Artifact Registry

Key Contributions & Impact

  • Built end-to-end Vertex AI + Kubeflow pipelines for preprocessing, training, validation, and controlled model promotion with GPU-backed workloads.
  • Containerized and deployed inference services using Docker and Vertex AI endpoints, enabling scalable batch and real-time predictions.
  • Implemented model monitoring and drift detection with Vertex AI and MLflow, ensuring traceability, performance tracking, and audit-ready experimentation.
