NLP-Based Escalation Prediction System

Built and deployed a production-grade NLP system to predict executive-level escalations from large-scale customer interaction data, including chat, IVR, and email transcripts. The solution leveraged ensemble machine learning models and transformer-based NLP techniques to identify high-risk cases early and enable proactive intervention.

Technology Stack

  • Languages & ML: Python, XGBoost, Transformer Models
  • Frameworks: Flask, Hugging Face, Scikit-learn
  • MLOps & Deployment: Kubeflow, Docker, MLflow
  • Cloud: Google Cloud Platform (App Engine)

Key Contributions & Impact

  • Performed extensive exploratory data analysis and feature engineering on chat, IVR, and email datasets to extract sentiment, intent, urgency, and temporal signals.
  • Developed and evaluated ensemble ML and transformer-based models, achieving up to 92% prediction accuracy on escalation classification tasks.
  • Designed and orchestrated end-to-end Kubeflow pipelines to automate data preprocessing, training, hyperparameter tuning, evaluation, and model versioning.
  • Implemented production-grade monitoring and drift detection to track model performance and prediction distributions over time.
  • Deployed scalable inference services on GCP App Engine, enabling real-time escalation risk scoring for operational workflows.
