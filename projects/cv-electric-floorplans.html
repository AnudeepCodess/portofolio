Electrical Point Detection from Floor Plans

Designed an object detection system to automatically identify and count electrical points from commercial floor plan images, eliminating manual counting.

Technology Stack

  • Computer Vision Tools: AutoML Vision, OpenCV
  • Cloud: Vertex AI

Key Contributions & Impact

  • Labeled and curated training datasets and trained AutoML Vision object detection models on Vertex AI.
  • Developed robust image preprocessing and postprocessing pipelines using OpenCV, including edge detection and morphological operations to improve detection accuracy.
  • Orchestrated training, deployment, and monitoring using Vertex AI and Kubeflow pipelines, enabling reproducible workflows and controlled releases.
